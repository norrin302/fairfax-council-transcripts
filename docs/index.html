About This Project

This site provides searchable transcripts of Fairfax City Council meetings. Transcripts are generated using AI speech recognition and include timestamps, speaker identification, and agenda item linking where possible.

Councilwoman Stacy Hall is proud to support transparency in local government. All meeting videos are public records from the City of Fairfax official archive .
